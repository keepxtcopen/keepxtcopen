# Closing Thoughts

> This project doesn't have some grand goal. It just pieces together information scattered across various corners of the Chinese internet.

---

I'm not affiliated with any competitor. I've just been following consumer electronics and open source for a long time, and I've come across a lot of discussion about Xiaotiancai.

These discussions are spread across Zhihu, Coolapk, Tieba, Bilibili comments, Smzdm... there's no central place for them. So I figured I'd just organize them.

## Why Keepxtcopen

xtc = Xiaotiancai's pinyin initials. keep ... open = keep it open and transparent.

Nothing profound. I just think these things should be brought out into the open.

Someone might ask:

> Xiaotiancai has **never** been open, so why "keep"?

My explanation is: Xiaotiancai used to be relatively open (the v1 era), though not in an absolute sense.

Of course, if you want to argue, I'll just say the project name doesn't matter.

## On Fairness

I don't guarantee 100% accuracy for everything written here. I'm not inside Xiaotiancai — a lot of the information comes from user feedback, industry common sense, and logical reasoning.

If you're from Xiaotiancai and think I've got something wrong, feel free to correct me.

But remember: **It's not my job to prove Xiaotiancai is innocent. It's your job to prove Xiaotiancai has nothing wrong.**

If you can actually open-source the kernel, revise the user agreement, remove the unjustified ban mechanism, and open up the social protocol — I'll be the first to delete this project and apologize.

## On "Why Not Just Sue"

People often say: "If you don't like it, sue them." "Just don't buy it." "Nobody's forcing you to buy it."

Honestly, these arguments overlook a basic fact:

**For an ordinary consumer facing a company with a huge market share, the cost of asserting rights is extremely high.**

- Legal avenues: time, money, energy
- Not buying: your child gets excluded from the social circle (this isn't something a child can choose)
- Putting up with it: something feels wrong, but you can't quite put your finger on what

The purpose of this project isn't to "take down Xiaotiancai." It's to take those things that "feel wrong but you can't quite articulate" and put them into words that everyone can see and understand.

## What's Next

- Relevant laws and regulations change

It'll all be updated here.
