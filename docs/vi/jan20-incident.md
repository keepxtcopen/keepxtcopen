# Su kien 20/1: Thay doi lon tinh nang Ban be

> Ngay 20 thang 1 nam 2026, Xiaotiancai khong co bat ky thong bao nao, da thuc hien mot loat thay doi doi voi tinh nang Ban be. Nguoi dung hoan toan khong co su chuan bi tam ly, thuc day phat hien moi thu da thay doi hoan toan.

Day khong phai sua loi, day la cat bo tinh nang nguoi dung dang co. Va thuc hien trong tinh trang nguoi dung khong biet, khong dong y.

---

## Thay doi 1: Thu hep pham vi hien thi like cua Ban be

**Truoc khi thay doi:** Bat ky ai like trong Ban be, ban deu co the thay. Du ban khong phai ban be cua nguoi dang bai, khong quen nguoi like, like do van hien thi o do.

**Sau khi thay doi:** Chi co the thay like cua **ban be chung**. Like cua nguoi khong phai ban be chung bi an di.

Be ngoai la de "bao ve quyen rieng tu", nhung thuc chat la cat bot mot tinh nang von mo, co tinh chat xa hoi.

Y nghia cua Ban be la gi? La de ban be thay su tuong tac lan nhau. Ban giau like cua nguoi khong phai ban be chung di, thi y nghia ton tai cua Ban be mat di mot phan lon.

**Va khong co bat ky lua chon nao.** Khong co cong tac, khong co thiet lap, khong co tuy chon "co muon nguoi khong phai ban be thay like hay khong". Cat mot nhat, thay doi truc tiep.

---

## Thay doi 2: Xoa ban be thi like bien mat

**Truoc khi thay doi:** Bai viet ban dang, nguoi khac like cho ban. Cho du sau ban xoa nguoi do khoi ban be, like cua ho van con.

**Sau khi thay doi:** Xoa ban be, tat ca like ma nguoi do tung like cho ban deu bi huy.

Thay doi nay anh huong lon nhat den nhung tre thich suu tam like. Ban co the mat hang tram like ma ban da mat cong gop duoc, boi vi ban voi ai do gian nhau xoa ban be -- mat, mat het.

Day khong phai gioi han ky thuat, day la co tinh thiet ke nhu vay.

Ban ngam ma xem: **Xoa ban be = Xoa luon lich su tuong tac.** Logic nay dat vao WeChat, QQ, Instagram, khong mot san pham mang xa hoi nao lam vay ca. Boi vi like la du lieu phu thuoc cua noi dung, khong phai la du lieu phu thuoc cua "quan he ban be".

Tai sao Xiaotiancai lai thay doi nhu vay? Loi giai thich duy nhat hop ly la: **Kich thich nguoi dung khong de dang xoa ban be, duy tri "do ket dinh" cua moi quan he ban be.** Hoac don gian la khong suy nghi, cho rang "xoa roi thi khong nen hien thi".

Du la nguyen nhan nao, hau qua deu do nguoi dung ganh chiu.

---

## Thay doi 3: Doi ten the quyen loi thanh "Genius PIN", huy bo chuc nang tang

**Truoc khi thay doi:** Nguoi dung co the nhan the quyen loi qua cac hoat dong khac nhau, va co the tang the quyen loi cho ban be.

**Sau khi thay doi:** The quyen loi doi ten thanh "Genius PIN", khong con ho tro tang.

The quyen loi noi trang la nhung thu nguoi dung danh doi thoi gian hoac cong suc de co. Co the tang cho ban, o muc do nao do la quyen dinh doat doi voi "tai san" cua chinh minh. Bay gio truc tiep huy bo chuc nang tang, khac nao noi rang:

**"Nhung thu ban doi lay, cach dung do chung toi quyet."**

Doi ten thi khong co van de gi -- "Genius PIN" nghe cung chang hay hon -- nhung nhan co hoi doi ten de cat bo chuc nang tang, thi hoi kho chu.

---

## Thay doi 4: Chuc nang cap bac bi go bo vinh vien

Dong ho Xiaotiancai von co mot **he thong cap bac**. Nguoi dung nang cap thong qua su dung hang ngay, tuong tac, hoan thanh nhiem vu. Cap bac khong chi la danh dau danh tinh, ma con di kem voi mot so quyen loi va cam giac thanh tuu.

Doi voi nhung tre dung dong ho tu nho, cap bac la minh chung cho su truong thanh. Tu LV.1 tu tu len cap cao, qua trinh nay co the keo dai vai nam.

Roi Xiaotiancai noi go la go.

**Go bo vinh vien, khong phai tam dung, khong phai cai to, ma la truc tiep cat bo.**

Ban mat vai nam troi tich luy cap bac, mot dem ve khong. Khong co den bu, khong co giai phap thay the.

"Tuoi tho cua ca mot the he ket thuc tai day" -- loi nay khong phinh dai.

Doi voi nhung tre sinh sau nam 2010, 2015, cap bac, Ban be, like trong dong ho Xiaotiancai chinh la "QQ Khong gian" cua chung. Bay gio ban thu quay lai xoa het cap bac QQ cua the he 80, 90 xem? Xem co gay tran khong.

Nhung boi vi la do cua tre, nguoi lon cho rang "chang qua la cap bac thoi ma", nen cat la cat, khong ai len tieng cho chung.

---

## Thay doi 5: Thay the like trang chu bang "Dong vien the thao"

**Chuc nang truoc day:** Trang chu Ban be co chuc nang like, tuong tu nhu like trong ban be WeChat. Ban dang mot dong thai, ban be co the like cho ban, day la tuong tac xa hoi co ban nhat.

**Sau khi thay doi:** Like trang chu bi cat bo, thay the bang mot chuc nang goi la "Dong vien the thao".

Goi dong vien the thao, noi trang chi co the like cho cac dong thai lien quan den the thao. Ban muon like cho dong thai hang ngay cua ban be? Khong duoc. Bay gio chi "dong vien" the thao thoi.

Logic cua thay doi nay rat ro rang: **Xiaotiancai muon bien Ban be tu "nen tang xa hoi" thanh "nen tang diem danh the thao".**

Ho cho rang tre deo dong ho nen dung de the thao, suc khoe, chu khong phai xa hoi. Vi the cat bot chuc nang xa hoi, thay bang "dong vien the thao".

Nhung van de la -- **Day khong phai dieu phu huynh muon, cung khong phai dieu tre muon, chi la san pham quan ly nghi ra.**

Tre dung dong ho de tuong tac voi ban cung lop, ban chat la nhu cau xa hoi. Ban cu ep mot san pham xa hoi thanh san pham suc khoe, ket qua la chang ben nao duoc long: mat ban chat xa hoi, chuc nang the thao cung chang ai dung.

Va giong nhu nhung dieu truoc: **Truoc khi thay doi khong co thong bao, sau khi thay doi khong co tuy chon.**

---

## Van de cot loi: Khong co su dong y cua nguoi dung

Ba thay doi tren, moi cai lay rieng ra co the co nguoi cho rang "cung duoc, khong nghiem trong lam". Nhung dat chung lai voi nhau:

1. Thu hep pham vi hien thi like
2. Xoa ban be thi like bien mat
3. Huy bo chuc nang tang the quyen loi

**Ba thay doi, khong mot cai nao thong bao truoc cho nguoi dung. Khong mot cai nao cho nguoi dung quyen lua chon. Khong mot cai nao cho phep nguoi dung giu lai tinh nang cu.**

Loi giai thich chinh thuc cua Xiaotiancai la "da thuc hien toi uu dua tren phan hoi cua mot so phu huynh".

Mot so phu huynh.

Noi trang, san pham cua ban thay doi the nao, hoan toan phu thuoc vao "ai noi to hon". Mot nhom phu huynh noi "toi khong muon con minh thay like tu nguoi la", duoc, thay. Con nhung tre thuc su dang dung chuc nang nay nghi gi -- khong quan trong.

Thiet bi la con ban dang dung, khong phai nhung "phu huynh" kia dang dung.

---

## Tai sao noi "khong xem nguoi dung ra gi"

Diem chung cua ba thay doi nay la:

1. **Khong co bao truoc** -- Co the trong ban cap nhat cung khong de cap
2. **Khong co lua chon** -- Thay la thay, khong chap nhan cung phai chap nhan
3. **Khong co den bu** -- Tai san xa hoi nguoi dung tich luy truoc do (so like, the quyen loi) truc tiep mat gia tri hoac bien mat

Neu day la mot cong ty nho binh thuong, ban co the noi "ho khong biet". Nhung Xiaotiancai chiem thi phan lon trong thi truong dong ho tre em, ban bao toi ho khong biet quan ly san pham, khong biet giao tiep voi nguoi dung? Khong phai khong biet, la khong quan tam.

Trong mat ho:
- Nguoi dung thuc su cua dong ho la **phu huynh** (nguoi tra tien)
- Tre em chi la **nguoi su dung** (khong co quyen quyet dinh)
- Vi vay chi can phu huynh khong len tieng, tre nghi gi cung mac

Logic nay ve mat thuong mai co le dung, ve mat dao duc khong dung duoc.

## Hau ky

Bai viet nay duoc viet vao nam 2026. Neu ban doc no khi da qua lau, co the Xiaotiancai da thay doi tro lai, hoac con lam toi te hon.

Du the nao, logic dung sau nhung thay doi nay khong thay doi: **Nguoi dung khong so huu du lieu cua minh, khong so huu moi quan he xa hoi cua minh, khong so huu quyen loi minh dat duoc. Xiaotiancai so huu tat ca.**
