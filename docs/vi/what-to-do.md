# Toi nen lam gi

> Chi che thoi khong du, phai biet co the lam gi. Duoi day phan theo tung doi tuong va nhung viec co the lam.

---

## La nguoi tieu dung thong thuong

### 1. Can nhac truoc khi mua

Neu ban dang can nhac mua dong ho Xiaotiancai cho con, hay tu hoi minh vai cau hoi:

- **Ban mua dong ho de lam gi?** Neu chi de dinh vi va goi dien, tren thi truong co nhieu dong ho lam duoc chuc nang nay, khong nhat thiet phai chon Xiaotiancai
- **Trong lop con ban co phai ai cung dung Xiaotiancai khong?** Neu co, ban phai chuan bi tam ly -- neu mua thuong hieu khac, con ban co the khong the ket ban qua dong ho voi ban cung lop
- **Ban co chap nhan thiet bi khong do minh kiem soat khong?** Thiet bi Xiaotiancai co kha nang kiem soat tu xa, ve ly thuyet nha san xuat co the bat cu luc nao quyet dinh thiet bi cua ban lam duoc gi va khong lam duoc gi
- **Ban co chap nhan rang mot ngay nao do tinh nang bi cat, tai khoan bi khoa ma khong co cho de noi ly khong?** Thoa thuan nguoi dung thien ve cong ty, kenh bao ve quyen loi co han

Nhung cau hoi nay khong co cau tra loi chuan. Moi nguoi moi hoan canh khac nhau. Nhung it ra ban nen biet nhung dieu nay truoc khi mua, chu khong phai sau khi mua moi phat hien.

### 2. Neu da mua roi

- **Dung giao hoan toan quyen app phu huynh cho con.** Viec kiem soat thiet bi Xiaotiancai cuoi cung duoc thuc hien qua phia phu huynh, tai khoan phu huynh la quyen cao nhat
- **Thuong xuyen kiem tra thoa thuan nguoi dung va chinh sach bao mat co cap nhat khong.** Mac du khong ai ngay nao cung xem, nhung it ra khi cap nhat phien ban lon hay luot qua mot chut
- **Dung thu root hoac cai ung dung khong chinh thuc.** Trong chinh sach hien tai, dieu nay se dan den khoa tai khoan va hau nhu khong co kenh khieu nai
- **Co van de hay giu lai bang chung truoc.** Anh man hinh, quay video, ghi lai hoi thoai voi cham soc khach hang, phong khi can bao ve quyen loi se dung den

### 3. Can nhac cac lua chon thay the

Neu ban xac dinh khong muon mua Xiaotiancai, tren thi truong con co cac lua chon sau (khong phai khuyen nghi, chi liet ke):

- **Dong ho tre em Huawei** -- He sinh thai tuong doi mo, nhung xa hoi cung chi gioi han trong thiet bi Huawei
- **Dong ho Xiaomi Mitu** -- Huong gia ca phai chang, chuc nang tuong doi co ban
- **Dong ho tre em 360** -- Nguoi choi kha lau nam, thi phan khong bang Xiaotiancai
- **Cac thuong hieu khac thuoc BBK** -- Uhm, thuc chat cung mot nha voi Xiaotiancai

> Noi that, hien nay thi truong dong ho tre em Trung Quoc ve co ban la moi hang choi mot kieu trong viec ket noi xa hoi. Day khong phai van de cua rieng Xiaotiancai, ma la van de cua ca nganh. Chi la Xiaotiancai chiem thi phan lon nhat va lam quyet nhat.

## La nha phat trien / Nguoi yeu thich ma nguon mo

### 1. Bao cao vi pham GPL cho GNU FSF

Dong ho Xiaotiancai su dung nhan Linux nhung khong cong khai ma nguon. Ban co the bao cao cho **Free Software Foundation** hoac **Software Freedom Conservancy** (SFC).

Trang web GNU co trang GPL tuan thu rieng:

- **GNU GPL Compliance** -- https://www.gnu.org/licenses/gpl-compliance.html
- **Software Freedom Conservancy** -- https://sfconservancy.org/

Khi bao cao can cung cap:

- Model thiet bi va phien ban Android di kem
- Bang chung thiet bi su dung nhan Linux (vi du anh man hinh so phien ban nhan)
- Bang chung nha san xuat khong cung cap ma nguon (vi du tim kiem tren trang web chinh thuc va GitHub khong ra ket qua)

SFC trong qua khu da thuc day nhieu cong ty (nhu LG, Samsung, Cisco) tuan thu GPL. Mac du ho chu yeu tap trung vao thi truong nuoc ngoai, nhung bao cao bang tieng Trung va tieng Anh deu duoc tiep nhan.

Neu ban cung muon tham gia chung toi, hay fork du an nay, mo Pull Request.

### 2. Theo doi va thuc day tren GitHub

- Tim kiem issue lien quan den "xiaotiancai kernel" hoac "imoo kernel source"
- Neu co nha phat trien dang thuc hien phan tich nguoc hoac phan tich nhan, hay theo doi va tham gia
- Neu mot ngay nao do Xiaotiancai cong bo ma nguon nhan, hay kiem tra tinh tuan thu

### 3. Giup nguoi dung thong thuong hieu van de ky thuat

- Tren dien dan giup nguoi khac hieu GPL la gi, tai sao dong ma nguon co van de
- Dich thuat va pho bien noi dung ky thuat lien quan
- Neu co kha nang, co the thu phan tich firmware thiet bi va cong khai phat hien

## La nguoi dung bi dam bay khoa tai khoan

### 1. Truoc het xac nhan minh da vi pham quy tac nao

Noi that, nhieu luc nguoi dung tu minh cung khong biet tai sao bi khoa. Cac truong hop thuong gap:

- Cai dat APK qua adb
- Thu gian quyen root
- Lien ket bot
- Thuc hien ky thuat dao nguoc va goi API <- (trai nghiem cua tac gia)
- Bi he thong nham lan (it gap hon)

**Nhung van de chinh la: nhung "quy tac" nay khong duoc viet ro trong thoa thuan nguoi dung.**

Vi vay ban truoc het la "bi phat boi mot quy tac ban khong biet".

### 2. Kenh bao ve quyen loi

**Buoc 1: Lien he cham soc khach hang**

Mac du kha nang cao la khong co ket qua, nhung day la tien de de di cac kenh khac -- ban can chung minh ban da lien he nha san xuat thuong luong roi.

- Duong day nong cham soc khach hang Xiaotiancai: 400-610-3999
- Giu lai tat ca ban ghi am cuoc goi va lich su tro chuyen

**Buoc 2: Khieu nai 12315**

Duong day khieu nai nguoi tieu dung thong nhat toan quoc. Co the tim kiem tieu trinh "12315" trong WeChat de khieu nai truc tiep.

Noi dung khieu nai:

- Thiet bi da mua bi han che su dung binh thuong do nha san xuat don phuong xac dinh vi pham
- Thoa thuan nguoi dung khong liet ke ro cac quy tac dan den khoa tai khoan
- Khoa tai khoan khien thiet bi khong the su dung binh thuong, anh huong den viec lien lac va an toan cua tre

**Buoc 3: Khieu nai Bo Cong Thuong**

Neu lien quan den han che chuc nang truyen thong, hoac buoc tren khong giai quyet duoc, co the khieu nai len Trung tam tiep nhan khieu nai nguoi dung vien thong Bo Cong Thuong.

Trang web: https://www.chinatcc.gov.cn/

**Buoc 4: Phoi gian truyen thong**

- Dang bai tren Black Cat Khieu nai (https://tousu.sina.com.cn/)
- Dang bai tren Tri Huc (Zhi Hu) ke lai trai nghiem cua ban
- Lien he cac blogger hoac truyen thong ve cong nghe so

Xiaotiancai rat quan tam den hinh anh thuong hieu. Khieu nai ca nhan co the khong ai quan tam, nhung neu chu de gay sot, ho se chiu ap luc.

### 3. Kha nang tap the bao ve quyen loi

Van de khoa tai khoan dong ho tre em khong phai ca biet. Neu ban bi khoa, kha nang cao con nhieu nguoi cung canh ngo nhu ban.

Co the thu:

- Tim nguoi cung canh ngo tren Kuan, Tieba, Tri Huc
- Tap the khieu nai len 12315 hoac Bo Cong Thuong (khieu nai tap the hieu qua hon khieu nai ca nhan)
- Neu co nguon luc luat su, co the tu van ve dieu kien khoi kien tap the

## Cuoi cung

Bao ve quyen loi rat kho, toi biet.

Doi thu cua ban khong phai mot nhan vien cham soc khach hang, khong phai mot cua hang, ma la mot cong ty co doi ngu phap ly, doi ngu quan he cong chung, chiem hon nua thi truong.

Nhung neu khong lam gi, ket qua la: **Ho se mai nghi rang lam vay khong co van de.**

Chi can mot nguoi boi vi xem du an nay ma dua ra quyet dinh khac, thi du an nay da co y nghia ton tai.
