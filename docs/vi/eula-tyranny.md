# Phan tich dieu khoan ap che trong thoa thuan nguoi dung

> Phan tich duoi day dua tren phien ban ngay 26 thang 6 nam 2023 cua thoa thuan nguoi dung app Xiaotiancai va thoa thuan cap phep phan mem nguoi dung cuoi (EULA). Neu ban phat hien thoa thuan da duoc cap nhat, hoan nghenh dong PR.

---

## Ket luan truoc

Thoa thuan nguoi dung cua Xiaotiancai tu than no khong phai la toi te nhat trong nganh. Nhung co mot vai van de thuc su ton tai.

Bo thoa thuan nay ket hop lai truyen tai mot thong diep: **Ban mua thiet bi, nhung thiet bi duoc su dung the nao, co tiep tuc su dung duoc hay khong, cuoi cung la do ho quyet dinh.**

---

## Phien ban thoa thuan

- **Thoa thuan nguoi dung app Xiaotiancai**: Ngay cap nhat 26 thang 6 nam 2023
- **Thoa thuan cap phep phan mem nguoi dung cuoi (EULA)**: Ngay cap nhat 26 thang 6 nam 2023
- **Lien ket chinh thuc**: https://static.watch.okii.com/watch/user_agreement/index.html

## 1. Quyen don phuong sua doi thoa thuan

Nhieu thoa thuan nguoi dung co dieu khoan nay, Xiaotiancai cung khong ngoai le.

**Dieu 19 cua thoa thuan app** nguyen van:

> Xiaotiancai co the sua doi thoa thuan nay bat cu luc nao dua tren viec cap nhat dich vu. Cac dieu khoan thoa thuan sau khi cap nhat se thay the ngay lap tuc cac dieu khoan thoa thuan cu.
>
> Neu viec cap nhat dich vu hoac thoa thuan nay anh huong nghiem trong den nguoi dung hoac han che nghiem trong viec truy cap hoac su dung dich vu, chung toi se thong bao cho ban trong mot khoang thoi gian hop ly truoc khi thay doi.

Thoa thuan noi rang thay doi quan trong se duoc thong bao truoc. Nhung van de la:

- "Anh huong nghiem trong den nguoi dung" tieu chuan nay do ai dinh nghia? Ro rang la chinh cong ty
- "Khoang thoi gian hop ly" la bao lau? Thoa thuan khong noi ro
- Hinh thuc thong bao la gi? Thoa thuan cung khong noi ro
- Cai goi la "thong bao" trong thuc te chi co the la cap nhat trang o mot goc nao do, nguoi dung hoan toan khong biet

Cai goi la "thong bao truoc", trong thuc hanh, co the chi la hinh thuc.

## 2. Pham vi thu thap du lieu

Dieu 5 cua thoa thuan liet ke pham vi chuc nang cua app, bao gom dinh vi, truyen thong xa hoi, the thao suc khoe, quan ly ung dung, v.v. Chi tiet cu the ve thu thap, luu tru, chia se duoc neu trong chinh sach bao mat.

> Lien ket chinh sach bao mat: https://static.watch.okii.com/watch/privacy_explain/index.html

Doi voi thiet bi tre em, van de chinh van la:

- Tinh can thiet va nguyen tac toi thieu trong thu thap co duoc thuc hien khong?
- Cac bien phap bao mat du lieu co dam bao khong?
- Khong co ben thu ba kiem toan, ben ngoai khong the xac minh.

## 3. Quyen cham dut dich vu

**Dieu 18 cua thoa thuan app (Cham dut hoac tam dinh chi boi Xiaotiancai):**

> Chung toi co the, theo quy dinh phap luat ap dung, tam dinh chi, han che, huy bo hoac cham dut quyen truy cap toan bo hoac mot phan dich vu cua ban ma khong phai chiu bat ky trach nhiem nao doi voi bat ky ca nhan hoac ben thu ba nao.
>
> Trong cac truong hop sau, chung toi co the ngay lap tuc tam dinh chi, han che, huy bo hoac cham dut quyen truy cap toan bo hoac mot phan dich vu cua ban ma khong can thong bao truoc:
> - (a) Ban vi pham hoac Xiaotiancai co ly do de cho rang ban sap vi pham thoa thuan nay
> - (b) Ban hoac bat ky nguoi nao thay mat ban thuc hien hanh vi gian lan hoac bat hop phap
> - (c) Theo yeu cau tu co quan thuc thi phap luat hoac chinh phu theo quy trinh phap ly hieu hanh
> - (d) De thuc hien bao tri hoac cap nhat he thong hoac phan cung khau cap
> - (e) Vi cac ly do ky thuat, bao mat hoac kinh doanh khong the luong truoc

Hay chu y cach dung tu o dieu (a) -- **"Xiaotiancai co ly do de cho rang ban sap vi pham thoa thuan nay"**, tieu chuan nay rat thap. Khong can ban thuc su vi pham, chi can cong ty "cam thay" ban sap vi pham, la co the cham dut dich vu.

Va cham dut dich vu khong can thong bao truoc cho ban, cung khong chiu bat ky trach nhiem nao. Hau qua cua viec cham dut dich vu la gi, phi tong xu ly the nao, thoa thuan khong noi ro.

**Dieu 14 cua EULA (Gioi han trach nhiem):**

> Tong gioi han boi thuong cho moi ton that cua ban tu Xiaotiancai khong vuot qua so tien ban da tra de mua dong ho thong minh Xiaotiancai.

Neu Xiaotiancai sai, ho boi thuong nhieu nhat bang tien mua mot chiec dong ho.

## 4. Hanh vi bi cam theo thoa thuan

**Dieu 4 va Dieu 7 cua EULA** cam ro rang cac hanh vi sau:

> Khong duoc sao chep, thao ra, thuc hien ky thuat dao nguoc, dich nguoc, thao do, hoac tao san pham phai sinh tu phan mem nay.
>
> Khong duoc co gang pha huy, vo hieu hoa, thay doi, huy bo hoac tranh khoi bat ky he thong quan ly quyen ky thuat so nao lien quan den phan mem.
>
> Khong duoc thuc hien bat ky hanh vi gay hai nao cho an ninh mang may tinh, bao gom nhung khong gioi han: tham do, quet, kiem tra diem yeu, can thiep, pha hoat hoat dong binh thuong khi khong duoc phep.

Root (gian quyen he thong), dich nguoc, vo hieu hoa gioi han he thong -- nhung hanh vi nay trong EULA co dieu khoan cam tuong ung.

**Van de la:** Thoa thuan cam la "dich nguoc, vo hieu hoa DRM" va cac hanh vi ky thuat khac, nhung trong thuc te thi hanh ly do khoa tai khoan la "cai app ben thu ba" hoac "root thiet bi" -- hai ben co tuong quan, nhung thoa thuan khong noi thang rang "ban root thi toi se khoa tai khoan". **Hau qua khoa tai khoan tu no khong duoc thong bao ro rang trong thoa thuan.**

Ngoai ra, **Dieu 9 cua thoa thuan app** liet ke cac hanh vi bi cam bao gom:

> Sao chep, thay doi, thuc hien ky thuat dao nguoc, dich nguoc, thao do, co gang xuat ma nguon, giai ma

Cac dieu khoan nay o cap do ky thuat da bao phu hanh vi root va sua doi he thong.

### Nhung co mot mau thuan quan trong: Giao thuc ma nguon mo

**Dieu 1, khoan (2) cua EULA viet ro rang:**

> Dong ho thong minh Xiaotiancai co the chua cac thanh phan phan mem duoc bao ve boi ban quyen ben thu ba va duoc ben thu ba cap phep theo giao thuc ma nguon mo... Khi ban su dung cac thanh phan ma nguon mo nay, cac dieu khoan va dieu kien cua giao thuc ma nguon mo se duoc ap dung. Thoa thuan nay khong lam thay doi bat ky quyen hoac nghia vu nao cua ban theo cac giao thuc ma nguon mo do. **Neu cac dieu kien cap phep trong thoa thuan nay xung dot voi noi dung cua giao thuc ma nguon mo lien quan den bat ky thanh phan ma nguon mo nao, thi giao thuc ma nguon mo se duoc uu tien.**

Thoa thuan tu noi: **Thanh phan ma nguon mo tuan theo giao thuc ma nguon mo, EULA khong quan ly duoc.**

Dieu nay dan den mot so van de:

**Thu nhat, chinh Xiaotiancai khong tuan thu giao thuc ma nguon mo.** Lop duoi dong ho la nhan Linux (GPLv2), theo yeu cau cua GPL phai cong khai ma nguon. Xiaotiancai khong dang. Tu minh khong tuan thu giao thuc ma nguon mo, lai dung EULA de han che nguoi dung sua doi phan cung duoc bao ve boi GPL, dieu nay khong hop ly.

**Thu hai, root thuc ra chi sua doi phan lon la phan ma nguon mo.** Ngoai mot so module hook sau nay, ramdisk.cpio, module nhan, phan boot -- nhung thu nay ho la dan xuat cua nhan Linux, hoac la thanh phan cua Du an Nguon mo Android (AOSP). Giao thuc cap phep cua chung (GPL, Apache 2.0, v.v.) von da cho phep nguoi dung sua doi. Xiaotiancai dung EULA de cam nguoi dung sua doi cac thanh phan ma nguon mo nay, tu mau thuan voi dieu thoa thuan da viet "giao thuc ma nguon mo duoc uu tien".

**Thu ba, co y go bo phan mem ma nguon mo.** Vi du nhu Yiketable -- mot phan mem ma nguon mo truoc day co trong cua hang ung dung, bi go bo voi ly do hoat dong danh cho nha phat trien tre tuoi ket thuc, nhung thuc chat la vi Xiaotiancai tu phat trien ra "san pham thay the" ma nguon dong. Day khong phai kiem duyet an ninh ky thuat, ma la kiem duyet vi mo hinh kinh doanh.

Vay bo logic nay thuc chat la: **Khi co loi cho minh thi vien EULA ra, khi bat loi cho minh (giao thuc ma nguon mo) thi lam nhu khong thay.**

## 5. Giai quyet tranh chap

**Dieu 20 cua thoa thuan app:**

> Thoa thuan nay duoc ky ket tai Dong Quan, tinh Quang Dong, Trung Quoc. Neu phat sinh tranh chap trong qua trinh thuc hien hop dong, cac ben se giai quyet bang thuong luong; neu thuong luong khong thanh, cac ben se giai quyet tai Toa an co tham quyen tai noi ky ket hop dong bang hinh thuc kien tung.

Thoa thuan viet ro toa an co tham quyen la o Dong Quan, giai quyet bang kien tung khong phai trong tai. Neu ban thuc su muon bao ve quyen loi, ban phai khoi kien tai toa an Dong Quan.

Doi voi nguoi dung khong o Quang Dong, chi phi kien tung o dia phuong khac la rat cao. Day cung la van de dien hinh cua dieu khoan mau -- an dinh dia diem giai quyet tranh chap tai noi cong ty dat tru so.

## 6. Quyen so huu tai khoan

**Dieu 7 cua thoa thuan app:**

> Quyen so huu tai khoan thuoc ve Xiaotiancai, ban chi co quyen su dung tai khoan trong pham vi cho phep boi Xiaotiancai.

Tai khoan ban dang ky, quyen so huu thuoc ve cong ty. Ban chi co "quyen su dung". Ve ly thuyet, cong ty co the thu hoi tai khoan cua ban vi bat ky ly do gi.

---

## Bo sung

Khong phai noi Xiaotiancai phai la mot ngoai le ve mat phap ly. Cac dieu khoan thien ve cong ty la rat pho bien trong nganh.

Nhung van de la:

1. Day la **thiet bi danh cho tre em**, nguoi quyet dinh mua va nguoi su dung la hai doi tuong khac nhau (phu huynh mua, tre em dung)
2. Muc do kiem soat vuot xa thiet bi thong minh thong thuong
3. Khi co su co, anh huong khong chi la thay dien thoai -- cong cu lien lac cua tre bi cat dut hoan toan

Nhung dieu nay nhin chung lai, khong phai cu noi "nganh nao cung the" la xong duoc.
