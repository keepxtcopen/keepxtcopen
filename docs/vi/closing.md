# May loi muon noi

> Viet du an nay khong co muc tieu lon lao gi. Chi la gop nhung thong tin roi rac o cac goc khac nhau cua Internet Trung Quoc lai voi nhau.

---

Ban than toi khong phai nguoi cua bat ky doi thu canh tranh nao, chi la theo doi linh vuc dien tu tieu dung va ma nguon mo trong mot thoi gian kha dai, dan dan thay rat nhieu cuoc thao luan ve Xiaotiancai.

Nhung cuoc thao luan nay phan bo khap Tri Huc, Kuan, Tieba, Binh luan Bilibili, Smzdm... Chung khong co mot noi tap trung. Toi nghi don gian la thu sap xep lai.

## Tai sao goi la keepxtcopen

xtc = viet tat cua Xiaotiancai. keep...open = giu minh bach cong khai.

Khong co y nghia sau xa gi. Chi cam thay nhung dieu nay nen duoc bay len mat ban.

Co nguoi co the hoi

> Xiaotiancai **chua bao gio** mo ca, tai sao lai keep?

Toi giai thich the nay: Xiaotiancai tung co thoi ky tuong doi mo (thoi dai v1), mac du khong phai la mo mot cach tuyet doi.

Dien nhien ban cu bien minh the nao cung duoc, toi chi co the noi, ten du an nay khong quan trong.

## Ve cong bang

Nhung thu viet trong du an nay, toi khong dam bao 100% chinh xac. Toi khong phai nguoi noi bo cua Xiaotiancai, nhieu thong tin den tu phan hoi nguoi dung, kien thuc thong thuong nganh, suy luan logic.

Neu ban la nguoi cua Xiaotiancai, thay toi viet khong dung, hoan nghenh chi ra.

Nhung hay nho: **Toi khong co nghia vu chung minh Xiaotiancai khong co van de. Ban co nghia vu chung minh Xiaotiancai khong co van de.**

Neu ban thuc su co the cong khai ma nguon nhan, sua doi thoa thuan nguoi dung, huy bo co che khoa tai khoan bat hop ly, mo giao thuc xa hoi -- toi se la nguoi dau tien xoa du an nay va xin loi ban.

## Ve "tai sao khong truc tiep khoi kien"

Thuong co nguoi noi: "Thay khong tot thi di kien di", "co ban dung mua la duoc", "co ai bat ban mua dau"

Noi that, nhung cau noi nay deu bo qua mot su thuc co ban:

**Nguoi tieu dung binh thuong doi dien voi mot cong ty chiem thi phan lon, chi phi bao ve quyen loi la rat cao.**

- Con duong phap ly: thoi gian, tien bac, cong suc
- Khong mua: con bi loai khoi vong tron xa hoi (day khong phai dieu tre co the chon)
- Chiu dung: luon cam thay co gi do khong on, nhung lai khong noi ro duoc

Muc dich cua du an nay khong phai la "danh do Xiaotiancai". Ma la viet lai nhung thu "cam thay sai sai nhung khong noi ro duoc" thanh van ban, de moi nguoi deu co the thay va hieu.

## Sau nay

Du an nay se tiep tuc duoc cap nhat. Neu:

- Xiaotiancai sua doi thoa thuan nguoi dung (du la tot hon hay xau hon)
- Co truong hop khoa tai khoan moi
- Co nguoi tim duoc bang chung thuc te ve ma nguon nhan
- Phap luat lien quan co thay doi

Deu se cap nhat vao day.
