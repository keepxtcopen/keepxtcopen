## Tim hieu nhanh

Neu ban lan dau nghe den nhung dieu nay, day la phien ban rut gon:

**Xiaotiancai da lam gi:**

1. He dieu hanh dong ho dua tren Android (nhan Linux), nhung chua bao gio cong khai ma nguon nhan theo yeu cau cua GPL -- dieu nay co van de ve mat phap ly
2. Thoa thuan nguoi dung chua nhieu dieu khoan thien ve cong ty, bao gom quyen don phuong sua doi thoa thuan, han che quyen bao ve quyen loi cua nguoi dung, v.v.
3. Thiet bi co he thong kiem soat dam may, phat hien root hoac cai app ben thu ba se truc tiep khoa tai khoan, va thoa thuan khong neu ro dieu nay
4. He thong tro chuyen chi cho phep thiet bi Xiaotiancai ket ban voi nhau, dung vi the thi truong de tao rao can xa hoi, thuc chat la bat coc moi quan he xa hoi cua tre em
