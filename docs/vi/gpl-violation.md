# Vi pham GPL: Dong ma nguon nhan Android

> Khong phai "ho co the da vi pham", ma la "ho thuc su khong tuan thu GPL".

---

## Truoc het, GPL la gi?

GPL (GNU General Public License) la mot giai phap ma nguon mo. Noi mot cach don gian:

- Ban co the su dung mien phi ma nguon dua tren giao thuc GPL
- **Nhung neu ban sua doi ma nguon va phan phoi san pham, ban phai cong khai ma nguon da sua**
- Nhan Linux su dung GPLv2, day la yeu cau bat buoc, khong phai dieu co the thuong luong

Dong ho Xiaotiancai chay he dieu hanh Android, ma lop duoi cua Android chinh la nhan Linux. Dieu nay co nghia la neu ban dung nhan Linux de tao san pham va ban ra, ban phai cung cap ma nguon nhan (bao gom phan ban da sua).

Day khong phai vung xam. Chinh Google cung ngoan ngoan dang ma nguon nhan Pixel len [kernel.google.com](https://kernel.google.com). Samsung da dang, Xiaomi da dang (du cham), OPPO cung da dang.

## Xiaotiancai khong dang

Cho den thoi diem du an nay ton tai, Xiaotiancai chua tung cong khai ma nguon nhan cua bat ky thiet bi nao tren bat ky kenh cong cong nao.

Ban len trang web cua ho tim, khong thay.
Ban len GitHub tim "xiaotiancai" hoac "imoo kernel", khong thay.
Ban len danh sach thu dang ky nhan, cung khong co.

Day khong phai van de "khong tim thay" -- ma la ho chua bao gio dang.

## Tai sao noi day la vi pham

Dieu khoan cua GPLv2 ghi rat ro:

> If you distribute copies of such a program, you must also make the source code available.

Ban ban dong ho co cai nhan Linux ra thi tuc la "distribute copies", ban phai cung cap ma nguon. Khong co ngoai le.

Mot so cong ty bien minh rang "dung nhan chua sua", nhung ban van phai cung cap ma nguon. Huong chi dong ho Xiaotiancai chac chan da sua -- ho phai thich ung voi SoC cua Spreadtrum hoac cac giai phap khac, phai lam driver, phai tinh chinh phan cung, khong the khong sua.

## Tai sao nhieu cong ty khong dang

Day la van de thuc te. Trong so cac nha san xuat thiet bi Android noi dia, khong chi rieng Xiaotiancai khong tuan thu GPL. Nhieu nha san xuat nho, hang tram trang cung khong dang. Nhung dieu nay khong co nghia la dung.

Chi la truoc day khong ai de y den phan loai dong ho tre em ma thoi.

## Tac dong thuc te la gi

Khong cong khai ma nguon nhan co nghia la:

1. **Khong the nghien cuu bao mat.** Co backdoor khong? Co lo hong khong? Khong ai co the kiem dinh
2. **Khong the co ROM ben thu ba.** He dieu hanh goc te ban cung phai chiu
3. **Vong doi thiet bi hoan toan do nha san xuat nam giu.** Ho bao ngung cap nhat, thiet bi cua ban chet
4. **Muon tu sua loi?** Cua dong

Mot thiet bi danh cho tre em, trong trang thai hop den, khong cho phep bat ky ai kiem tra ma nguon ben duoi cua no.

Ban thay dieu nay co thich hop khong?
